About

Hi there, I'm Casey Burridge.

I got into web design about a year ago, when I decided to build my blog, Talking Mandarin.
Since then, I've come a long way and worked on a lot of websites.
I specialise in building simple, clean and good looking Wordpress websites. I like to work with my client's vision and brand in mind. If you already have an idea of how you want your site to look, I can help bring your ideas to life. If, on the other hand, you leave the design to me, I'll happily work to create something that I'm confident you'll love. Nothing will be finalised untill you are happy and satisfied.
I invite you to get in touch, and we can discuss your website.
